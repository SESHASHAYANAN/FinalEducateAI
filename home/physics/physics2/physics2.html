Introduction Of Physics!

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand the laws of nature and the interactions between matter and energy. It covers topics like mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, and modern physics. Physics employs the scientific method to discover the principles governing the natural world, leading to technological advancements that shape our modern society.
