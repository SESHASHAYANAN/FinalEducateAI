Lecture 2: What is Physics!

Physics is the fundamental science that explores the nature of matter, energy, space, and time. It seeks to uncover the laws and principles governing the physical world, from the smallest subatomic particles to the largest structures in the universe. Physics forms the foundation for understanding the natural world and enabling technological advancements that transform our modern society.
